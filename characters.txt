Anya Petrova (from "The Glass City"):
A young, fiercely independent hacker living in a dystopian future where information is heavily controlled. She uses her skills to rebel against the oppressive regime, often putting herself in danger to expose the truth.
Dr. Silas Thorne (from "The Shadowed Clinic"):
A brilliant but troubled neurosurgeon who is haunted by a past medical malpractice case. He becomes involved in controversial experimental treatments, blurring the lines between healing and obsession.
Kai Chen (from "The Dragon's Legacy"):
A seemingly ordinary teenager who discovers he is the last heir to a powerful ancient dragon lineage. As he learns to control his newfound abilities, he must navigate the dangerous world of mystical creatures and a secret society seeking his power.
Eliza Bellweather (from "The Gilded Cage"):
A spirited, rebellious socialite living in the Victorian era who challenges societal norms by disguising herself as a man to pursue her passion for science and explore the forbidden corners of the male-dominated world.
Gabriel Stone (from "The Wanderer"):
A solitary, enigmatic wanderer with a mysterious past who travels through different dimensions, collecting artifacts and unraveling ancient secrets. He possesses a unique ability to see glimpses of the future, often leading him to pivotal moments in history.
